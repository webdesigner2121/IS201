Goals

Setting appropriate goals is key when beginning your journey in real estate investing.

You should be modest in your expectations when it comes to invest in real estate. Be sure to be very specific in the type of propreties that you are seeking and include details such as how much you are willing to pay, how much are you willing to invest to renovate and how much cash flow you want your property to produce.

An example of that might look like this: I will purchase 4 properties per year. My maximum purchase price is $300 000. I will use $25 000 to upgrade the property. I wish to cash flow $250 a mount.
